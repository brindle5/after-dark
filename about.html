About us

After Dark Tours was founded in 2008 by Ian and Debbie. They met on a ghost hunt in 1996 and after eleven years running the Museum of the Occult in Torquay, moved to Stratford-upon-Avon. Between them, they have several decades’ experience of running guided tours that show visitors the creepy, disturbing, and downright macabre side of Shakespeare’s hometown. Ian started off as an IT professional and became interested in the paranormal when he bought a small cottage that turned out to be haunted. Debbie trained as a hairdresser but dabbled in spirituality with tarot reading and crystal therapy. The idea for After Dark Tours came from her interest in Charlotte Clopton, the 16th century heiress who was buried alive in the family crypt- (you hear her story in the Murder and Mayhem tour!).
