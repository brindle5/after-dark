Our Tours

Ghosts of Stratford

Stratford-upon-Avon, a pretty market town based in the heart of Warwickshire, is famous for being the birthplace of playwright William Shakespeare. But Stratford isn’t just quaint Tudor buildings and tea shops, it has a dark past. On our ‘Ghosts of Stratford’ tour, we take you on a 2 hour walk around the centre of town while one of our knowledgeable guides regales you with stories of some of Stratford’s most famous hauntings.
Tour length: 3km
Price: £12.00 adult; £7.00 child; OAP/student £10.00
Days and times: Nightly at 6pm, 8pm, 10pm & midnight. (closed bank holidays)

Murder and Mayhem

Stratford-upon-Avon has seen its fair share of murders, maimings, and grisly events. On this gripping 90-minute bus tour*, we take in the sites of these disturbing incidents. From a ritualistic murder at Meon Hill, to a deadly quarrel at Compton Verney, and the slaughter of a family in Wooten Wawen, this tour brings you close to the darker side of human nature.
Price: £14.00 adult; £10.00 child**; OAP/student £12.00
Days and times: Nightly at 6.30pm, 9pm, 10.30pm, and midnight (closed bank holidays)
*Tour route may vary in accordance with local traffic restrictions and road closures
**This tour is not suitable for children under 10 years old.

About us

After Dark Tours was founded in 2008 by Ian and Debbie. They met on a ghost hunt in 1996 and after eleven years running the Museum of the Occult in Torquay, moved to Stratford-upon-Avon. Between them, they have several decades’ experience of running guided tours that show visitors the creepy, disturbing, and downright macabre side of Shakespeare’s hometown. Ian started off as an IT professional and became interested in the paranormal when he bought a small cottage that turned out to be haunted. Debbie trained as a hairdresser but dabbled in spirituality with tarot reading and crystal therapy. The idea for After Dark Tours came from her interest in Charlotte Clopton, the 16th century heiress who was buried alive in the family crypt- (you hear her story in the Murder and Mayhem tour!).
